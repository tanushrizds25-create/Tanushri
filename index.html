About SBJITMR

Established with a vision to impart quality technical education, SBJITMR focuses on industry-ready curriculum, research innovation and value-based learning.

The institute offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Engineering and Management. It is equipped with modern laboratories, digital classrooms, research centers and an active training & placement cell.

With strong academic governance and experienced faculty members, the institute promotes innovation, entrepreneurship and collaborative research.
